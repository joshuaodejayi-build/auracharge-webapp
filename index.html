The Problem

The EV charging Safety Void

While the UK's public charging network expands rapidly in quantity, it is failing in quality of experience. EV charging requires 30–60 minutes of dwell time in isolated, often dark retail parks. Existing platforms like Zap-Map and Google Maps tell you if the plug works. Nobody tells you if you'll be safe.

AuraCharge fills this gap by quantifying environmental security into a navigable metric, transforming "Invisible Risk" into "Actionable Safety." We align directly with the UK Government's National Strategy to Tackle Violence Against Women and Girls (VAWG) and the PAS 1899 EV charging safety mandate.

AuraScore

A 120-point safety score.
Dynamically calculated. Real-time.

Our proprietary multi-layer intelligence system synthesises verified crime data, AI-driven visual audits, and real-time community reporting into a single, actionable safety score for every charging station in the UK.

Visual Security
CCTV coverage density, signage presence, emergency help buttons. Verified via AI-powered image analysis (proprietary AI vision analysis).
Proprietary weighted scoring
Illumination
Task and perimeter lighting levels. Applies a 0.5× penalty multiplier instantly if primary LEDs are reported broken, reflecting infrastructure decay in real-time.
Proprietary weighted scoring
Human Presence
Quantifies "Natural Surveillance." Sites earn bonuses for proximity (within 50m) to 24/7 staffed lobbies or shops, translating social safety into a defensible numerical value.
Proprietary weighted scoring
Environmental Design
Evaluates site visibility using CPTED (Crime Prevention Through Environmental Design) principles. Rewards open sightlines and penalises hidden alcoves.
Proprietary weighted scoring
Historical Risk
Processes verified crime data through our proprietary risk normalisation engine, isolating location-relevant incidents to produce a fair and accurate safety signal.
Proprietary weighted scoring
PAS 1899 Compliance
Tracks adherence to the 2026 UK Government Mandate for accessible and safe charging. Makes AuraCharge a vital B2B compliance tool for Charging Point Operators.

Guardian Mode

Your digital bodyguard.
Always watching.

Guardian Mode is AuraCharge's proprietary Dead Man's Switch protocol. If you feel unsafe while charging and fail to check in, AuraCharge autonomously escalates — from SMS to voice call to emergency services — without you needing to do anything.

Dead Man's Switch Logic
Proprietary fail-safe protocol. No response = automatic escalation through our secure cloud infrastructure.
SMS + Voice Escalation
Live GPS location and site safety context sent to your designated emergency contacts instantly via secure messaging.
Live GPS + AuraScore Context
Every SOS message includes your precise coordinates and the site's current AuraScore — giving responders actionable intelligence, not just a location pin.
VAWG Alignment
Directly supports the UK Government's National Strategy to Tackle Violence Against Women and Girls by providing active monitoring in isolated charging environments.
